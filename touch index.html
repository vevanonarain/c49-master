Following are some of the origami designs:

Design 1 - Jaguar

Facts about Jaguars

1)Mass: 56 – 96 kg (Adult)

2)Scientific name: Panthera onca

3)Height: 63 – 76 cm (Adult, At Shoulder)

4)Lifespan: 12 – 15 years (In the wild)

5)Speed: 80 kilometres per hour / 50 miles per hour

Design 2 - Hippopotamus

Facts about Hippopotamus

1)Mass: Male: 1,500 – 1,800 kg (Adult), Female: 1,300 – 1,500 kg (Adult)

2)Scientific name: Hippopotamus amphibius

3)Length: 3.3 – 5.2 m (Adult)

4)Lifespan: 40 – 50 years

5)Speed: 30 km/h

Design 3 - Rhino

Facts about Rhino

1)Mass: White rhinoceros: 2,300 kg, Black rhinoceros: 800 – 1,400 kg, Indian rhinoceros: 2,200 kg

2)Scientific name: Rhinocerotidae

3)Height: White rhinoceros: 1.7 – 1.9 m, Black rhinoceros: 1.3 – 1.8 m, Indian rhinoceros: 1.6 – 1.9 m

4)Lifespan: White rhinoceros: 40 – 50 years, Black rhinoceros: 35 – 50 years, Indian rhinoceros: 35 – 45 years

5)Speed: White rhinoceros- 50 km/h, Black rhinoceros- 55 km/h, Indian rhinoceros- 55 km/h

Design 4 - Horse

Facts about Horse

1)Mass: 380 – 1,000 kg (Adult)

2)Scientific name: Equus caballus

3)Height: 1.4 – 1.8 m

4)Lifespan: 25-30 years

5)Speed: 88 km/h (Maximum, Sprint)

Design 5 - Eagle

Facts about Eagle

1)Mass: Bald eagle: 3 – 6.3 kg, Golden eagle: 3 – 7 kg

2)Scientific name: Haliaeetus leucocephalus

3)Length: Golden eagle: 66 – 100 cm, Harpy eagle: 99 cm

4)Lifespan: Bald eagle: 20 years, Crowned eagle: 14 years

5)Speed: Bald eagle: 120 – 160 km/h (Maximum, Sprint)
